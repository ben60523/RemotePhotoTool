RemotePhotoTool User Interface

RemotePhotoTool gives you control over your remotely connected camera and shows you the live viewfinder.

HDR Photo Mode

RemotePhotoTool makes it easy to take bracketed photos and starts up Photomatix to process the bracketed shots further.

Panorama Photo Mode

RemotePhotoTool helps you taking panorama shots and starts up Hugin to process the images further.

Autoplay handler

RemotePhotoTool integrates with Windows and lets you start RemotePhotoTool when a new camera is connected.

Device Properties

RemotePhotoTool lists the device properties of the connected camera.

Image Properties

RemotePhotoTool lists the properties of the camera to be used in the next shot.

Connect Camera dialog

RemotePhotoTool shows you the currently connected cameras. The list is updated as cameras are connected and unplugged.

Camera infos

RemotePhotoTool can collect diagnostic information of your camera, e.g. for finding errors or testing camera models.

Settings dialog

RemotePhotoTool lets you modify settings, such as folder name generation, logging or external tools.
